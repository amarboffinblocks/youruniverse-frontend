## Submitting a Complaint

Users who wish to file a complaint can do so by contacting our customer support team
through [support@youruniverse.ai](mailto:support@youruniverse.ai), or via the “Contact” link at the bottom of the
[YourUniverse.AI](http://youruniverse.ai/) site.

## Information to Include in the Complaint

When submitting a complaint, users are encouraged to provide the following details to help us
investigate and address the issue promptly:

a) User's username, full name and best e-mail to reply to;

b) Description of the complaint, including relevant details such as the date and time of the
incident;

c) Any supporting documentation or screenshots, if applicable.

## Documentation of Complaint Review Process

a) Receipt and Acknowledgment: Upon receiving a complaint, we will acknowledge receipt
within 24-48 hours. This acknowledgment will include a reference number for the complaint
and the name of the person handling the complaint.

c) Decision-Making: The decision-making process will be carried out by a designated
Complaints Officer or a relevant team, depending on the nature of the complaint. This process

d) Documentation: Every step of the complaint review process, from receipt to resolution, will

## Escalation

If a user is dissatisfied with the resolution provided, they may request further escalation. In
this case, users can notify our support team within a reasonable timeframe, providing clear
reasons for their dissatisfaction with the initial resolution.

The case will be reviewed by individuals or teams not initially involved in the complaint
resolution process. They will reassess the complaint and reconsider the previous decision. The
user will be notified of the outcome of the escalation within a reasonable time-frame.
